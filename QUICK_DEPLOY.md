## Cost Optimization

**Initial**: 56 cloudlets (~$35/month)  
**Optimized**: 24-32 cloudlets (~$15-20/month)

**Reduce costs after installation:**
1. Go to Node.js node settings
2. Reduce from 32 to 8-16 cloudlets
3. Save up to 50% on hosting costs
